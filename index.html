Restaurant Menu Generator

This application exists to help you build an HTML food menu. It's entirely free too! Just fill out the form options and click the "Save Menu" button when you are done. You can even see a preview of your current progress at any time by clicking on the preview window on the upper right hand corner of your screen. The menu you create will be saved in your browser until you click the "Reset Menu" button in the top left hand corner of your screen.

Nothing you enter into this form will ever be saved anywhere, except for on your own computer. All of the work is done on your browser which means there is no need for any of your information to go to a server.
